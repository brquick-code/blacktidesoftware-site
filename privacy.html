Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

Death Atlas ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Death Atlas mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Location Information

Death Atlas may request access to your device location while the app is in use.

Location data is used solely to:

  • Display your position on the map
  • Improve map navigation features
  • Provide directions via Apple Maps or Google Maps

We do not store or sell your location data.

Location data is processed locally on your device and is not permanently retained by us.

B. Subscription & Purchase Information

Death Atlas offers optional premium subscriptions.

Purchases are processed through:

  • Apple App Store (iOS)
  • Google Play Store (Android)

We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status.

We do not collect or store:

  • Credit card numbers
  • Billing addresses
  • Payment information

All billing is handled securely by Apple or Google.

We may receive anonymous subscription status data from RevenueCat to determine whether premium features should be unlocked.

C. Basic App Usage Data

We may collect limited, anonymous technical data such as:

  • App version
  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Crash reports

This data is used solely to improve app stability and performance.

We do not use advertising trackers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Death Atlas does not:

  • Require user accounts
  • Collect names or email addresses
  • Sell personal data
  • Share personal data for advertising
  • Track users across other apps or websites

3. Third-Party Services

The app may link to external services including:

  • Apple Maps
  • Google Maps
  • Wikipedia
  • Other public source websites

These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their data practices.

RevenueCat privacy policy:
https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple privacy policy:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Google privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect any limited data processed by the app.

Because purchases are processed through Apple and Google, sensitive financial data is never stored on our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Death Atlas is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Choices

You may:

  • Disable location access in your device settings
  • Cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID or Google Play account
  • Delete the app at any time

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" above.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

support@deathatlas.com
