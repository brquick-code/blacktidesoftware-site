Featured App

Death Atlas

Death Atlas is an interactive map-based app that helps users explore historical deaths, disappearances, burial locations, and notable events. It combines geography, history, and research into a single discovery experience for users interested in the stories tied to real places.

Available on Google Play. Additional platform support and updates are in progress.
